The Arizona State University Innovation Open once again showcased the power of innovation, accessibility and sustainability in the heart of Phoenix!

At ASUio, 25 teams from 24 universities came together to showcase their ventures in front of industry leaders and technology developers for the chance to win nearly $400,000.

A special congratulations to the winners of the prizes:...
ATOMICS: Molecular-based data storage methods
Somnair: Neuromodulating device for obstructive sleep apnea
Oxylus: Carbon conversion technology
BreatheEV: Reservation-based scheduling for electric vehicle charging
Ecobrew: Sustainable methods for alcohol fermentation
Oleo Sustainable Palm Solutions Inc.: Sustainable palm oil alternatives
Reel Free: Oxygen tubing retraction device
Trinity Tube: Minimally invasive gastric feeding tube for premature babies
Each of these ventures embodies the spirit of innovation and holds the potential to create positive change in our world. A huge thank you to our sponsors, partners and everyone who made the event a resounding success! Together, we're building a brighter future fueled by innovation and sustainability. #winASUio
